Commutative Algebra Module Theorem

Implementation across multiple languages and frameworks

Theorem Statement

Let A be a Noetherian ring, M a finitely generated A-module. Then:

⋂p ∈ Ass(M) p = √(Ann(M))

Where:

  • Ass(M): Associated primes of M
  • Ann(M): Annihilator ideal of M
  • √I: Radical of ideal I
